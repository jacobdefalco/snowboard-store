Origin Story

Snow 'n Stuff was not always the bustling snowboard store it is today. In fact, it started as a small operation in a rural Vermont town called Snowsville. The owner of the store, Snowey McSnowerson, was a decorated US Army Veteran who had been forced to leave the military due to an injury sustained during a deployment in Afghanistan. He struggled to find his footing in civilian life, and turned to alcohol and drugs to cope with the PTSD he had developed from his time in combat.

Snowey's life took a turn for the worse when he found himself in trouble with the cartel. He had been living in a large city, and had gotten involved in some illegal activity in order to make ends meet. When the cartel found out about his involvement, they threatened his life and he was forced to flee the city and start over in a new place.

Snowey chose Snowsville because it was a small town where he could hide from the cartel and start fresh. He began to participate in a life of organized crime in order to survive, but he knew that it was not the life he wanted to lead. One day, he discovered his new love, snowboarding. He had never tried it before, but when he strapped on a snowboard and flew down a mountain, he felt alive for the first time in years.

From that moment on, Snowey made it his mission to bring the joys of snowboarding to the masses. He opened Snow 'n Stuff, a small snowboard shop in the heart of Snowsville. At first, the store struggled to make ends meet, but Snowey was determined to succeed. He poured all of his energy and resources into the store, and soon it began to thrive.

Snowey became a beloved figure in the Snowsville community, and his store became a hub for snowboarders from all over the region. He hired a team of passionate and knowledgeable staff members, and together they helped to build a snowboarding culture in the area. They hosted events, gave lessons, and sold high-quality snowboarding gear to anyone who walked through the door.

Today, Snow 'n Stuff is a staple of the Snowsville community. Snowey has long since left his life of organized crime behind, and he is now known as a champion of snowboarding and a beloved figure in the town. He continues to run the store with the same passion and dedication he had when he first opened it, and he is proud to have brought the joys of snowboarding to so many people over the years.
